Air Fried Crumbed Fish

Description

Crumbed fish is one of my favorite fried items, and this air-fried version of the recipe gives me great flavor without the fat.
Prep:10 mins
Cook:12 mins
Total:22 mins
Servings:4
Yield:4 fillets

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 4 flounder fillets
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Step

    2. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).

  2. Step

    3. Mix bread crumbs and oil together in a bowl. Stir until mixture becomes loose and crumbly.

  3. Step

    4. Dip fish fillets into the egg; shake off any excess.
    Dip fillets into the bread crumb mixture; coat evenly and fully.

  4. Step

    5. Lay coated fillets gently in the preheated air fryer.
    Cook until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 12 minutes. Garnish with lemon slices.
